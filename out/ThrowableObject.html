Class: ThrowableObject

ThrowableObject()

new ThrowableObject()

Represents a throwable object in the game that extends the MovableObject class. This class simulates the behavior of an object being thrown, including its initial position, size, and motion. The object is thrown with an initial speed and affected by gravity, moving horizontally over time. It rotates during flight, and splashes when hitting an enemy or surface.
